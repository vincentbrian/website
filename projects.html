Final Project

The final project for this illustration class was to create a children's book. The book had to be illustrated in any style you wanted and the story content had to be well thought out and revised. Also, there had to be a minimum of three complete spreads. The rest of the pages had to be fully drawn out but not colored. The timeline for this project was two weeks.
